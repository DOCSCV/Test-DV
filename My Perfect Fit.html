Professional Preferences

    I thrive in team-based, integrated care environments that promote NP/PA collaboration and multi-specialty practices. Working in an integrated health system with on-site specialists in fields like cardiology and endocrinology is preferred.

    I'm seeking a role that offers a CME allowance of $5,000+, at least 1 week of CME time, and funding for conferences and professional growth. Clear leadership tracks for roles such as Medical Director or Chief Medical Officer are important for my long-term goals.
